MS EXCEL DASHBOARD

Click to view project details...

Created a dashboard that analyzes bike sales data. The data contains income, age, commute distance and other contextual information for hundreds of customers from across different regions. Analysis was done in Microsoft Excel.

Data Insights

• Male gender purchased more bikes compared to the female gender.

• Customer with higher income purchased more bikes compared to the lower income customers.

• Middle aged customers purchased more bikes compared to other age brackets.

• Shorter commute distance purchsed more bikes compared to other distances.

Recommendations from Insight

• Company's advertisement budget should be slightly raised to acquire more customers.

• Advertisement campaigns should be targeted at male gender and middle aged customers.

TABLEAU DASHBOARD

Click to view project details...

Created a dashboard that analyzes revenue from sales. The data contains revenue per month, revenue per age, revenue per state, revenue per gender and other contextual information for thousands of customers from across different regions. Analysis done in Tableau.

Data Insights

• More sales was recorded for the Month of December and April respectively. This could be as a result of the holiday seasons (Chrismas and Easter Holidays).

• The data also shows that, customers with age range (30-40) and (60-70) years had the highest purchase compare to other age range.

• The data also shows a positive correlation between discount on price and quantity demanded.

Recommendations from Insight

• Marketing department should design more engaging marketing campaigns during the month of December and April.

• Discounting should also be improved upon to drive more sales.
